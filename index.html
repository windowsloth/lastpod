About

This is a chart of connections between people/groups that have been covered on the Last Podcast on the Left. It's definitely an incomplete list, but I am working on adding to it over time. There are a lot of episodes to comb through, so this is represents just what I have listened to recently, and what I could remember off the top of my head.

Other Notes

You might notice that there are almost no Heavy Hitters included in in the chart. There's a handful of reasons for that.

First of all, the Heavy Hitters are for the most part lone wolves. There isn't much that actually ties them to anyone else on the chart in a meaningful way, so there's no point in including them.

The second reason is that when there is some kind of potential connection, it's usually something made up. This might seem like a weird sticking point, since a vast majority of the information this chart is based on could be called made up, but for me there's a difference Obvious Bullshit and Fun Bullshit.

For example, at the end of Ep. 100: Bundy Pt. 2, there is a very brief discussion about a theory that Bundy took the fall for a series of crimes committed by a young George Bush, but it is presented as a joke and not taken at all seriously.

On the other hand, most of Ep. 188: the Dulce Facility Pt. 2 deals with the fact that Dulce as described by Thomas Castello probably does not exist. The difference is that there is the better part of two episodes spent on the topic, and it also fits into the larger narrative of aliens and the Secret Government.

There's definitely a lot of gray area in there, but it's been working for me up to this point.
